Fremantle
2023-10-14
 Fremantle Harbour is a historic and vibrant maritime gateway that has played a pivotal role in the region's development and continues to be a hub of economic and cultural activity. Nestled at the mouth of the Swan River, Fremantle Harbour is an integral part of the port city of Fremantle and serves as one of Australia's most significant and busiest ports.
